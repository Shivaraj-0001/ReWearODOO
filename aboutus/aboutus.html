About NovaRewear

➤ Who We Are

NovaRewear is a student-built platform created for the Odoo Hackathon 2025. We aim to reduce fashion waste by helping users exchange unused clothing in a fun and meaningful way.

➤ Our Mission

We want to make sustainable fashion accessible. NovaRewear connects people who want to swap clothes or redeem items using points, encouraging a reuse-before-discard lifestyle.

➤ What You Can Do

  • List your unused clothes with details and images
  • Swap clothes directly with others
  • Earn points and redeem them on clothes you like
